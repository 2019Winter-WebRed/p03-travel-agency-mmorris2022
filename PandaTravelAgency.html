History

St. Kitts is also known as St. Christopher. St. Kitts was home to the first Caribbean British and French colonies in the mid-1620s. The capital of St. Kitts, and also its largest port, is the town of Basseterre. Their population is 54,821.Their dirving side is on the left.

Activities

There is lots of stuff to do on St. Kitts and Nevis islands. Some of them include:

  • Brimstone Hill Fortress
  • Oualie Beach
  • Royal St. Kitts Golf Club
  • St. Kitts Scenic Railway

Other things to do on St. Kitts and Nevis are: go swimming in the ocean or the bay, horseback riding, zipline, hiking up a volcant, visiting the local markets, relaxing by the pools, walking in a rainforest, and having fun!
